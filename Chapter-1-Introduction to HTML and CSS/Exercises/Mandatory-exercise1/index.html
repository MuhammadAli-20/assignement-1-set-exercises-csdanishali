Beef Nihari Recipe

Beef nihari is a mouth-watering dish that comprises a rich, slow-cooked stew filled with pleasant spices.

Ingredients of the beef nihari recipe:

  • 2 lb beef shank
  • 1 lb marrow bones (optional)
  • 4 tablespoons of cooking oil or ghee
  • 1 onion thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh ginger (thin strips)
  • ¼ cup of fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • 1 lime (squeezed)

Spices that are to be ground:

  • 1 tablespoon of black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon of coriander seeds
  • 1 tablespoon of cumin seeds
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 star anise
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 2 black cardamoms
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 piece of nutmeg
  • 1 mace

Spices that are to be added to the beef:

  • 1½ tablespoons of ground spices
  • 1½ or 1 tablespoon of red chilli powder
  • 2 tablespoons of turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1½ cups of water or as required
  • Add sugar at the end of the cooking

Method of making beef nihari step by step:

  1. Put a frying pan on top of the medium heat. Add all the spices to be ground in the pan.
  2. Stir all these spices together for about 2-3 minutes. Make sure they don't get burnt!
  3. As soon as the spices get toasted, remove them from the frying pan and wait until they cool.
  4. Now add the toasted spices inside the spice grinder in order to make a refined powder.
  5. This dish only requires 1½ tablespoons of the ground spice powder. Keep the rest of the powder in an airtight container.
  6. Heat cooking oil or ghee in a normal-sized pan.
  7. In the same pan, add ginger-garlic paste with sliced onions and cook them until they turn golden brown. After that, put them in the cooking pot.
  8. Put marrow bones and beef pieces into the cooking pot.
  9. Cook them on medium-high heat. Keep cooking them unless they turn brown.
  10. Now add the red chilli powder, ground spices, salt, and turmeric into the cooking pot.
  11. Mix the added ingredients in such a way that the marrow bones and beef pieces are entirely coated with the spices in the cooking pot.
  12. Next, pour adequate hot water into the cooking pot so that the water thoroughly covers the meat.
  13. Start heating the pot until the water inside begins to boil.
  14. Once the water begins to boil, immediately lower the heat of the stove.
  15. Afterwards cover the pot and let the beef nihari cook properly until the beef becomes tender.
  16. Take a small bowl.
  17. Put water and flour into it and stir well.
  18. Keep stirring until the mixture becomes slurry.
  19. Next, add the slurry mixture into the simmering nihari and stir it continuously.
  20. Cook everything together.
  21. Turn off the stove as soon as the gravy becomes thick.
  22. Enjoy the beef nihari with rice or roti.
