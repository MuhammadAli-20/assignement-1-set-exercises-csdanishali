Damsel

Overview of the Plot

Damsel (2024) is an adventurous movie filled with full of fantasy whose director is Juan Carlos Fresnadillo. Its core actress is Millie Bobby Brown, who plays the role of Elodie, a young lady who marries Prince Henry to assist her kingdom. She finds out a shocking secret when she reaches the land of Prince Henry. The plan of the royal family was to sacrifice Elodie to a dragon in order to fulfill an olden promise. It is necessary for Elodie to make use of her bravery and mind to run away. The story of the Damsel movie is quite different as compared with the fairy tales in which a princess depends on others to get rescued. Elodie fights for herself with bravery instead of depending on a hero.
